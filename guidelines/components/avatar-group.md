### AvatarGroup

**Purpose**: Stack of overlapping avatars.

**Import**: `import { AvatarGroup } from '@anthropic/laurel-design'`

**Example**:
```tsx
<AvatarGroup max={3}>
  <Avatar src="/user1.jpg" alt="User 1" />
  <Avatar src="/user2.jpg" alt="User 2" />
  <Avatar initials="JD" />
  <Avatar initials="AB" />
</AvatarGroup>
```
