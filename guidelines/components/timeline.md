### Timeline

**Purpose**: Vertical timeline for chronological events.

**Import**: `import { Timeline, TimelineItem } from '@anthropic/laurel-design'`

**Example**:
```tsx
<Timeline>
  <TimelineItem title="Order Placed" description="Jan 15, 2025" />
  <TimelineItem title="Shipped" description="Jan 17, 2025" />
  <TimelineItem title="Delivered" description="Jan 20, 2025" />
</Timeline>
```
