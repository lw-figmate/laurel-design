### Carousel

**Import**: `import { Carousel } from '@anthropic/laurel-design'`

Slideshow content carousel with navigation.

**Example**:
```tsx
<Carousel>
  <Image src="/slide1.jpg" alt="Slide 1" />
  <Image src="/slide2.jpg" alt="Slide 2" />
  <Image src="/slide3.jpg" alt="Slide 3" />
</Carousel>
```
